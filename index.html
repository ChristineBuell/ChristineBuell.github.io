Knitting Needle Sizes

One interesting quirk of knitting are the needle sizes; the way they're measured. It makes using older knitting patterns frustrating. Knitting needles now generally come in 2 measurements, either US or Metric. But if you go back a few decades you have the British measurements, which go in the other direction from the US.
