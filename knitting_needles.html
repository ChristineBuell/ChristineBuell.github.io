Needle Size Chart

Here is a sample of the needle sizes. You'll notice that there are some holes in the US size. US size needles don't have as many in between sizes as the metric do. Actually Japanese needle conversions are even worse. While the British and US line up with Metric somewhat, Japanese needles have sizes like 3.6mm and 4.2mm!
